About Me

Mechanical, electrical, and software engineering have changed the world before. How can they change it now?

My name is Enrique Flores and I am a graduate student at MIT. I believe that circular supply chains and manufacturing systems are the way we can create a better future. In particular, by embracing and developing Industry 4.0, AI and Advanced Process Control in these fields, we can do more with less, creating a world where we all thrive.
