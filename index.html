About me

Can mechanical, electrical, and software engineering change the world? This is the question that I am dedicating my life to answer.

My name is Enrique Flores and I am a graduate student at MIT. My focus is in the development of optimized, sustainable, and circular supply chains and manufacturing systems. I believe that, through the implementation of AI, Industry 4.0, and Advanced Process Control, we can do more with less, creating a world where we all thrive without exploiting resources.
